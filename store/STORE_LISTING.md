## Full description
PulsIQ reads your heart — resting rate, HRV, respiratory rate, sleep — and
turns it into plain-language coaching you can act on today. Every number is
shown against your own baseline, never as raw jargon.

WHAT YOU CAN DO
• Hold the pulse button and just talk — "grande oat latte and a burrito bowl."
  PulsIQ transcribes it on-device and logs it instantly.
• Watch the PulsIQ Score (0–100) blend cardiac recovery, sleep, fuel quality,
  and hydration into one number, with a live breakdown.
• Scan a menu with your camera and get the top 3 picks for long, steady energy.
• Get a gentle post-meal walk nudge after carb-dense meals, with a live timer.
• See an evening forecast for tonight's recovery that cites its strongest signal.

PRIVATE BY DESIGN
• Health data is encrypted on your device. Optional cloud backup stores only
  ciphertext — the key never leaves your phone.
• No passwords (Google Sign-In or passkeys), biometric app lock, and an
  in-app audit trail of every health-data access.
• No third-party analytics. Your data is never sold or shared.
• Export everything as JSON or delete it all, any time, in a couple of taps.

PulsIQ is a wellness companion, not a medical device. Nothing in the app is
medical advice — for health concerns, talk to a clinician.
